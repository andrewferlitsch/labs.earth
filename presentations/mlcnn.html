Introduction to Convolutional Neural Networks

Abstract: This workshop introduces basic concepts of convolutional neural networks. Concepts covered are image pixels, image preprocessing, feature detectors, feature maps, convolution, ReLU, pooling and flattening.

Workshop

  • MNIST - Convolutional Neural Network Part IV)
  • MNIST - Dropout (Part V)

Level: Fundamental / Intermediate (Code Along)

Requirements: No prior programming or statistics knowledge required. Some knowledge of neural networks is recommended.
