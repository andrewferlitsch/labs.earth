Introduction to Neural Networks

Abstract: This workshop introduces basic concepts of neural networks. Concepts covered are Neurons, Binary vs. Categorical vs. Real Value output, activation functions, fully connected networks, deep neural networks, specialized learners, cost function and feed forward.

Workshop

  • MNIST - Neural Network Part I)
  • MNIST - Fully Connected Neural Network Part II)
  • MNIST - Deep Neural Network (Part III)

Level: Fundamental / Intermediate (Code Along)

Requirements: No prior programming or statistics knowledge required.
